Experience

I am a self taught web developer and designer currently participating in FreeCodeCamp. I am familiar with responsive and mobile first design choices and have experience working with frameworks like Bootstrap. I am also currently learning more about improving workflow using tools such as Gulp, SASS and Git.

I also enjoy working in Adobe Illustrator and Sketch and have completed some small projects designing SVG illustrations and animating them using CSS and the GSAP Javascript library.

In the past I have also had experience working on online charity events, and have written copy for websites, managed social media accounts and designed posters and images for use in publicising events.

My background in history and museum studies has instilled in me a love for research and learning; and I am always on the lookout to put those skills into action. I enjoy researching new concepts and ideas, and discovering new solutions to problems.
